## Overview

The Products page shows all your products and allows you to view their details.

The Products domain also contains the following sub-sections:

- Collections and Categories for better product organization.
- Gift Cards to manage gift-card products associated with a specific customer.

## View List of Products

View the list of available products in your ecommerce store by clicking on Products from the sidebar menu.

In the list, find product details such as the name, collection, status, and more.
